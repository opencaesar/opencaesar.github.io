The following R&D projects are run by the Rigorous, Agile, and Intelligent Systems Engineering (RAISE) LAB. The lab carries multidisciplinary research applying Formal Methods (rigor), DevSecOps (agility), and AI (intelligence) techniques to modernize the domain of systems engieering. The PI of the lab is Prof. Maged Elaasar (the openCAESAR Lead). The R&D projects are research internships that are carried by (undergraduate or graduate) students from top-tier universities in the US and other countries, often in collaboration with co-Is from various (academic, corporate, or government) labs. If you are interested in collaborating, sponsoring, or leveraging the results of existing projects, or starting new ones, please contact Prof. Maged Elaasar to discuss possiblities.
